Will Poulter, also known as William Jack Poulter, is a well-known English actor. Among the films for which he has received praise are Son of Rambow, The Chronicles of Narnia: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader, We’re the Millers, The Maze Runner, The Revenant, Detroit, and Black Mirror: Bandersnatch.

We’re the Millers earned him two MTV Movie Awards, including one for Breakthrough Performance. Additionally, he was nominated for a BAFTA Rising Star Award for the same performance. Later, he won an MTV Movie Award for his performance in The Maze Runner. He was also nominated for multiple awards for his roles in Son of Rambow, The Chronicles of Narnia: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader, and Wild Bill.
