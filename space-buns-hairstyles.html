15 Ways to Wear Space Buns

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 7, 2024

If you think that high bun hairstyles only extend to topknots and messy buns, think again. Space buns (also known as high-puff hairstyles) have become one of the most popular hair trends. We aren't surprised as they are undeniably versatile. Whether you prefer a voluminous Boho-chic vibe or edgy and sleek is more your thing, space buns can take on multiple aesthetics. While we usually think of space buns as that go-to festival hairstyle, we're seeing celebrities and influencers adopt the hairstyle and make it their own for any occasion.

From different hair lengths to hair textures, anyone can (and should) do a space bun hairstyle. Ahead, we gathered 15 space bun hairstyles that will prove why this look will reign supreme in the high bun hairstyle universe.

Space Buns and Swirly Edges

Space buns match perfectly with styled edges. With all of the hair off your face, the swoops and swirls of perfectly laid baby hairs can be showcased and instantly transform your look.

Low Space Buns

This effortless look is both fun and classy. To replicate, part your hair into four sections. Take the two sections closer to the back of your head and coil each into a bun. Then, take the two remaining sections closer to your face and twist them around the base of the buns you just made. Secure with a bobby pin and you're good for summer.

Ribbon Space Buns

Millie Bobby Brown brought some sweetness to a red carpet event with her center-parted space buns. The addition of a black ribbon in each bun elevated the look.

Traditional Meets Half-Up Half-Down

Space buns on curly hair textures look amazing because of the natural volume. Try this half-up half-down hairstyle, which mimics the traditional two-bun look but also gives you a chance to show off your hair texture and length.

Slicked Space Buns

On the days you want to get your hair up and out of your face or wake up with major bedhead, try slicked space buns. Part your hair down the middle, and then gather each side into a high sleek pigtail. Once you're done, wrap the ends of your pigtail into a bun for a look that's as bomb as Lizzo's.

Accessorized Space Buns

Take inspiration from Janelle Monae and accessorize your space buns with some fun hair clips (ahem, butterfly clips are back) or a dainty headband.

Scrunchy Space Buns

Another easy way to dress up your space buns is with giant scrunchies. The scrunchy is a '90s trend that unsurprisingly made its way back into the limelight. So if you're into it, wrap each of your space buns in a satin scrunchy to accessorize your look.

Fanned-Out Space Buns

Doing space buns on short hair can be tricky because the ends tend to stick out. However, celebrity hairstylist Emma Willis, shows us an easy way to make those frayed ends work. The trick is to wrap the loose ends of your hair around your scrunchy and leave the rest poking out. The results are an adorable, Boho-chic space bun look.

Classic Space Buns

Tracee Ellis Ross shows us that traditional space buns can never disappoint. It's a reliable hairstyle that's cute and casual.

Vertical Space Buns

An innovative look we haven't seen yet but are totally into, is this voluminous hairstyle that stacks two buns on the back of the head for a unique half-up, half-down look.

Face-Framing Space Buns

Celebrity hairstylist, Chris Appleton, created an elegant, face-framing space bun hairstyle on J.Lo. We love that her face-framing pieces are super chunky, which gives versatility to the look.

Mini Buns

When Kourtney Kardashian was spotted laying poolside with two mini space buns, we decided that this is the only hairstyle we'll be doing for beach days moving forward.

Two-Toned Space Buns

Cardi B fully embraced that E-girl aesthetic from head to toe here. However, her hot pink and black space bun hairstyle was arguably the star of the show.

Braided Space Buns

Wrapping your braids into a space bun hairstyle is an easy way to add volume and texture to the look.

Buzz Cut Space Buns

Throwback to the 2013 VMAs when Miley Cyrus showed up wearing two mini space buns and the sides of her head shaved. She proved space buns are possible for short hair lengths, too.
