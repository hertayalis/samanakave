The Meaning Behind The Song: An Ests En Mis Sueos by Rata Blanca

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 18, 2024

I have always been fascinated by the power of music to evoke emotions and transport us to different worlds. There are songs that resonate with us on a deeper level, capturing certain moments or feelings in our lives. One such song that holds a special place in my heart is “Aún Estás En Mis Sueños” by Rata Blanca.

Released in 2005 as part of their album “La Llave de la Puerta Secreta,” this beautiful ballad is a testament to Rata Blanca’s talent for crafting heartfelt compositions. With its poetic lyrics and powerful melodies, “Aún Estás En Mis Sueños” explores themes of love, longing, and the enchanting nature of dreams.

The Lyrics

“Hoy desperté con un montón de marcas en mi piel
Alguien por la noche me las dejó
Quiero saber si esto fue la magia de tus labios
Hay perfume de mujer, flotando aquí
El conjuro de un cuento de amor
Ya me atrapó lo puedo sentir”

These opening lines set the stage for a tale of mystery and longing. The protagonist wakes up with marks on their skin, a reminder of a mysterious encounter from the previous night. There is a sense of curiosity and yearning to know if this was the result of the magic of a loved one’s lips—the lingering perfume, the enchantment of a love story that has captivated them.

“Y por las noches puedo sentir su calor
Su dulce magia, me hace perder la razón
Y de mis sueños creo que un día escapó
Para esconderse dentro de mi corazón”

The chorus beautifully captures the essence of the song. The protagonist confesses that, even during the nights, they can feel the warmth and sweet magic of their beloved. It overwhelms them to the point of losing their reason. They believe that this person once escaped from their dreams to find solace within their heart.

“Como escapar, nada real
Me importa conocer
Solo esperaré hasta dormir
Estoy aquí frente al gran espejo para convencer
A los duendes que dirán cómo llegar
A aprender el hechizo ideal
Que junte los sueños con la realidad”

The second verse delves into a sense of frustration and desperation. The protagonist realizes that escaping from this all-consuming love is futile. They are willing to wait until they fall asleep because, in that dream realm, they can seek guidance from the magical creatures and discover the ideal spell that will merge their dreams with reality.

“Y por las noches puedo sentir su calor
Su dulce magia, me hace perder la razón
Y de mis sueños creo que un día escapó
Para esconderse dentro de mi corazón”

The chorus returns, reinforcing the intensity of their emotions and the belief that their beloved resides within their heart, even if only in dreams.

The Meaning

“Aún Estás En Mis Sueños” speaks to the universal experience of longing for a love that feels both magical and unattainable. It captures the essence of dreaming about someone, feeling their presence, and the profound impact they have on our lives, even when they are physically absent.

The song reminds us that love can be an enchanting force that transcends reality. It explores the tension between dreams and the desire to bring them to life, highlighting the power of imagination and the possibility of merging the magic of dreams with the reality of love.

Whether you have experienced a similar longing or simply appreciate the beauty of Rata Blanca’s music, “Aún Estás En Mis Sueños” has a way of stirring the soul and resonating with the deepest parts of our being. It is a reminder that music has a special ability to touch us, transport us, and give voice to emotions that are otherwise hard to express.

So next time you find yourself lost in the enchanting melodies of “Aún Estás En Mis Sueños,” let it take you on a journey of love, longing, and the profound connection between dreams and reality.

**Written By Walter Giardino and Vocals By Adrián Barilari, “Aún Estás En Mis Sueños” was released on April 25, 2005, as part of Rata Blanca’s album “La Llave de la Puerta Secreta.”**
