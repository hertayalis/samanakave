Avatar Frontiers of Pandora : Frequency Puzzle Guide, How to Find It? - SarkariResult

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

News: “Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora” is an eagerly anticipated video game set to deliver an immersive and exhilarating gaming adventure. Among its notable challenges is the Frequency Puzzle, requiring players to employ the Systems Interrogation Device (SID) to troubleshoot maintenance nodes.

The “Welcome to the Resistance” Mission

As players undertake the central mission titled “Welcome to the Resistance,” their objective involves repairing maintenance nodes scattered across Resistance HQ. To achieve this goal, players utilize the Systems Interrogation Device (SID), a gun-like hacking tool. Upon activating the SID, players gain the ability to visualize power wires on the floor, providing a navigational guide to reach the generators.

The Mini-Game at Each Junction Box

At every junction box, players engage in a mini-game where they confront two circles—one green and one white. The task involves aligning the white circle with the green one by adjusting its size. As players progress, this challenge recurs at subsequent nodes, escalating in complexity with each iteration.

The Challenging Maze in the third Stage

During the third stage, participants encounter a heightened hacking challenge. They are tasked with maneuvering through a time-constrained maze comprised of white lines. The objective is to guide the icon along these lines and successfully reach the exit before the allotted time expires.

Completing the “Find the Frequency” Mission

Successfully resolving all nodes within the Frequency Puzzle accomplishes the mission known as “Find the Frequency.” Players are required to employ their problem-solving abilities to navigate intricate maze-like puzzles and advance in the game. This particular puzzle acts as a precursor to the more complex hacking challenges that players will face as they progress further in the game.

Equipping the SID

To equip the SID in the game, players can activate it by pressing L1 + △ on PlayStation, LB + Y on Xbox, or Numpad 5 on PC. Once the SID is activated, it reveals power wires on the floor, enabling players to follow them to the junction boxes and initiate the mini-games.

The introduction of the Frequency Puzzle in “Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora” adds an exhilarating and challenging dimension to the gaming experience. Utilizing the Systems Interrogation Device (SID) and successfully navigating through the mini-games allows players to advance in the main mission and unlock progressively intriguing puzzles as they explore the expansive world of the game. So, gear up, equip your SID, and prepare to immerse yourself in the thrilling universe of “Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora.”
