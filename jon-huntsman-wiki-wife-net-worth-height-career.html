Jon Huntsman- Wiki, Wife, Net Worth, Height, Career

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Jon Huntsman Jr. was an American businessman, politician, and diplomat who served as Utah’s governor for four terms. He has held the position of the American ambassador to China and Singapore.

Jon was a Republican who served in five US presidential administrations. He made a 2012 Republican presidential campaign and was named US Ambassador to Russia in 2017. Jon serves as both the chairman of the Huntsman Cancer Foundation and the CEO of the family-owned Huntsman Corporation.

What is the Net Worth of Jon Huntsman? Salary, Earnings

As of December 2022, Jon Huntsman Jr.’s net worth is predicted to be $1 billion. He has frequently received approval ratings that have contributed 90% of his net worth throughout his time in office.

He is the owner of foundations that have given hundreds of millions of dollars to good causes around the globe. His professional career has produced a sizable salary that increases quickly every year, making him wealthier over time.

Huntsman is a polite politician who has over the years kept up a good demeanor and a favorable outlook on the general population. He has contributed to several philanthropic organizations throughout the years and is actively involved in charitable activities.

Where was Jon Huntsman born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Redwood City, in the state of California, is where Huntsman was born. His father, Jon Huntsman Sr., was a business executive who founded the Huntsman Corporation, which produced generic Styrofoam cartons for McDonald’s and other fast-food chains and was one of the largest petrochemical companies in the United States, making him a billionaire.

His mother, Karen Huntsman, was the daughter of LDS Church apostle David B. Haight. Of his eight siblings, he is the youngest.

He went to Salt Lake City’s Highland High School but left before he could graduate to become a keyboardist for a rock band. He registered at the University of Utah and joined the fraternity, Sigma Chi.

Before moving to the University of Pennsylvania and earning a Bachelor of Arts in International politics, he served as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Taiwan for two years.

How tall is Jon Huntsman? Weight, Hair Color

As of 2022, Jon Huntsman Jr., who was born on March 26, 1960, will be 62 years old. He stands 1.78 meters tall and weighs 75 kilograms.

How did Jon Huntsman start his Professional Career?

Huntsman served as a White House aide during the administration of Ronald Reagan. He served as the United States Trade Representative’s deputy during Bush’s presidency but left to run for governor of Utah on the Republican ticket.

He won a second term as governor in 2004 and served for an additional four years. Later, during Obama’s administration, he was named US Ambassador to China.

He resigned in 2011 to run for president, but he later withdrew his name from consideration in favor of Mitt Romney. Jon was named head of the Atlantic Council in 2014.

He served as the CEO of Huntsman Family Holdings Company, chairman of the Huntsman Cancer Foundation, and executive of Huntsman Corporation. The Huntsman Cancer Institute, which his father established, appointed him as chairman.

In 2012, he was elected to the Ford Motor Company board of directors. Later, he was elected to the Caterpillar Inc. board. He served on the boards of the Brookings Institution, the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace, the Asia Society in New York, and the National Bureau of Asian Research before founding the Pacific Council on International Policy.
