Jude Law - Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 8, 2024

The English actor was born in London in 1972 and is best known for starring in The Talented Mr. Ripley, Cold Mountain, The Holiday, and more

    Jude Law's Early Life

    David Jude Heywarth Law was born on December 29, 1972 in London, England, the son of two teachers, but was referred to by his middle name, Jude. While growing up in London, he developed an early interest in acting and the theater.

    Jude Law's Personal Life

    While a prominent philanthropist and activist, Law prefers to keep his private life out of the spotlight. In 1997, he married his (1994) co-star Sadie Frost, with whom he welcomed two sons, Raff and Rudy, and daughter Iris, with Raff and Iris following in their parents' footsteps. Law and Frost divorced in 2003.

    He and co-star Sienna Miller began dating at the end of 2003, announcing their engagement the following year. However, it went downhill when Law released an apology after cheating on her with his children's nanny, and they ended things in 2006. He has since welcomed children with model Samantha Burke and Catherine Harding, before tying the knot once again with Phillipa Coan in 2019, who gave birth to their two children (his sixth and seventh).
