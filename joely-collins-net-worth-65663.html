Biography/Timeline

2004

Collins won the award for Best Actress in a Canadian Film at the Vancouver Film Critics Circle Awards 2004 for The Love Crimes of Gillian Guess.

2008

Collins married Dutch-born Stefan Buitelaar on August 23, 2008, in Leiden, The Netherlands. On October 26, 2009, Collins gave birth to their first child.

2009

Born and raised largely in Vancouver, British Columbia, Collins studied at the Vancouver Youth Theater, and later at London's Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts. She was named Canada's "Best Leading Actress" at the age of 22 for her work on the television series Madison. She appeared on the long-running drama Cold Squad. In 2009, she co-founded StoryLab Productions and produced the award-winning feature film Becoming Redwood.
