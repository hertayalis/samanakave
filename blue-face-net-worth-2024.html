Early Life and Career Beginnings

Blueface was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. He had a keen interest in music from a young age, but it wasn’t until after high school that he began to take his passion seriously. Blueface’s rise to fame was meteoric. His unique style and charismatic personality quickly caught the attention of the music industry. His breakout single, “Thotiana,” peaked at number 8 on the Billboard Hot 100, further cementing his place in the industry.

Albums and Singles
