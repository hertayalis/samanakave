Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Who Is Aya Cash?

Aya Cash is an American actress. She is best known for starring as Gretchen Cutler on the FX series You’re the Worst. Similarly, she is also known for her roles in Sleepwalk with Me and The Wolf of Wall Street.

Aya Cash- Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Aya Cash was born on July 13, 1982 in San Francisco, California, U.S. As of 2023, she is 41 years old. She is American by nationality and her ethnicity is caucasian. Similarly, her zodiac sign is Cancer.

Talking about her parents, she was born to Eugene Cash (father) and Kim Addonizio (mother). Her mother is a poet and novelist whereas her father is Eugene Cash.

Similarly, her grandmother Pauline Betz was a former tennis player and her grandfather Bob Addie was a sports writer. She might be a single kid of her parents as there is no info about her siblings.

Talking about her educational background, she went to the San Francisco School of the Arts. Later, she joined the University of Minnesota where she earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Acting in 2004.

Aya Cash-Professional Life, Careers

Talking about her career, Aya Cash has been working in the entertainment industry since 2006. She has worked on several films, television shows, and theatre.

She started her acting career in 2006 by playing the role of Janine Lesko in Law and Order. In the same year, she was seen on stage in The Pain and the Itch as Kalina.

In 2007, she appeared in television series such as Brotherhood as Martha Danners and as Chrissy in Spellbound. She was seen in the 2011 Fox comedy series Traffic Light as a regular cast Callie. She was featured in the FX Network series You’re the Worst as Gretchen Cutler from 2014 to 2019.

Moreover, she also made her appearance as Stormfront in the second season of the Prime Video superhero dark comedy-drama series The Boys which premiered in September 2020. Similarly, she also played the role of local newspaper reporter Cheryl Peterson in a pilot for a Fox sitcom titled This Country in 2020.

Furthermore, she made her movie debut in 2008 by playing the role of Olga in Off Jackson Avenue. She made her appearances such as 10 Crosby as Aya, All Exchanges as Final Dom, and Village People as Barbara.

Recently, she worked in the 2023 American drama film The Young Wife.
