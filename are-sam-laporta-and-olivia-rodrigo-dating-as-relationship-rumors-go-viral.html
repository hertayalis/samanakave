Sam LaPorta and Olivia Rodrigo’s alleged romance

LaPorta has already established himself as one of the league’s top tight ends in his debut campaign. With nine touchdowns, the Lion leads all players in his position. He’s supposedly already surpassed Kelce as the finest tight end in jokes.

As one of the best TEs, LaPorta should, of course, be dating a pop sensation. It shouldn’t come as a surprise because Olivia Rodrigo was the clear favourite from the start—the perfect one, at that.  Rodrigo is set to go on her own tour, and even at such a young age, she is already an established pop star. Are Sam and Olivia dating?

Tej Seth claimed sarcastically on December 16 that he would see how far social media rumours could go. “All Sam LaPorta needs is a celebrity girlfriend, and he’ll be considered the best tight end in the NFL,” he wrote. The joke was never taken too seriously, but many still found it funny. Since then, it has taken on a life of its own as word of Seth’s inappropriate tweet has spread.

Sam and Olivia lovers might be disappointed, but it appears that the singer is seeing Louis Partridge. Neither has officially confirmed it, though. Conversely, LaPorta and his college love are still going strong. It became official in December 2019 between him and Callie Dellinger. Despite coming from Illinois, they first connected as freshmen at Iowa.
