Pokemon Go Dark Flames Branching Timed Research Quest

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Trainers, the Dark Flames is underway, and you can now complete a new Branching Timed Research and earn Premium Battle Pass, XP, and encounters with Dark, and Fire-type Pokemon.

The Dark Flames brings two new Pokemon into the game Turtonator and Mega Sableye. Shiny Turtonator and Shiny Mega Sableye are also available to encounter. Sounds great, right? Well, make sure to participate and get yourself a new shiny Pokemon.

Now, back to the topic. The Dark Flames Branching Timed Research offers you two paths. You can choose between two paths, focused on Fire Pokemon and Dark Pokemon respectively. The tasks are not that hard to complete, and you can earn Premium Battle Pass, 15,000 XP, and encounters with event-themed Pokemon. Now, letâ€™s see the list of tasks below.

Dark Flames Branching Timed Research Quest

Dark Flames Timed Research 1/4

  • Explore 2km: 10 PokeBall
  • Catch 2 Fire-or Dark-type Pokemon: 10 Great Ball
  • Catch 10 different species of Pokemon: 10 Ultra Ball
  • Rewards: 3 Rare Candy, 5 Razz Berry, and 1000 Stardust

Dark Flames Dark-type Branching Path 2/4

  • Catch 20 Dark-type Pokemon: 10 PokeBall
  • Power up 10 Dark-type Pokemon: 10 Pinap Berry
  • Take 25 snapshots of wild Dark-type Pokemon: Carvanha
  • Rewards: 2500 XP, 15 PokeBall, and 1500 Stardust

Dark Flames Dark-type Branching Path 3/4

  • Purify 1 Dark-type Pokemon: 10 Great Ball
  • Power up 10 Dark-type Pokemon: Sneasel
  • Evolve 3 Dark-type Pokemon: Scraggy
  • Rewards: 2500 XP, Premium Battle Pass, and 2000 Stardust

Dark Flames Dark-type Branching Path 4/4

  • Catch 20 Dark-type Shadow Pokemon: 3 Silver Pinap Berry
  • Power up 10 Dark-type Pokemon: 10 Ultra Ball
  • Battle in 3 Raids: Houndoom
  • Rewards: 7500 XP, 50 Litten Candy, and 2500 Stardust

Dark Flames Fire-type Branching Path 2/4

  • Catch 20 Fire-type Pokemon: 10 PokeBall
  • Power up 10 Fire-type Pokemon: 10 Pinap Berry
  • Take 25 snapshots of wild Dark-type Pokemon: Slugma
  • Rewards: 2500 XP, 15 PokeBall, and 1500 Stardust

Dark Flames Fire-type Branching Path 3/4

  • Purify 1 Fire-type Pokemon: 10 Great Ball
  • Power up 10 Fire-type Pokemon: Alolan Marowak
  • Evolve 3 Fire-type Pokemon: Darumaka
  • Rewards: 2500 XP, Premium Battle Pass, and 2000 Stardust

Dark Flames Fire-type Branching Path 4/4

  • Catch 20 Fire-type Shadow Pokemon: 3 Silver Pinap Berry
  • Power up 10 Fire-type Pokemon: 10 Ultra Ball
  • Battle in 3 Raids: Houndoom
  • Rewards: 7500 XP, 50 Litten Candy, and 2500 Stardust

The Pokemon Go Dark Flames starts on June 29, 2023, at 10 AM, and ends on July 2, 2023, at 8 PM local time.
