Celebrities Who Are Serial Cheaters

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Before tiger blood, goddesses, and an HIV diagnosis, Charlie Sheen attempted to be a normal, married man ... except, well, fidelity wasn't his strong suit.

In 2005, Sheen was rumored to have cheated on his then-wife of just two years, Denise Richards, prompting her to file for divorce when she was seven months pregnant with their second child. Sheen and his rep adamantly denied the cheating allegations.

He gave marriage another go with the oft-troubled Brooke Mueller, but that didn't end well either. In 2010, Radar Online and In Touch both reported that he was entertaining paid sex workers, notably an alleged escort named Angelina Tracy, during his marriage to Mueller, sometimes in their marital bed. Sheen was spotted more than once leaving Tracy's home, wearing a fake mustache at least once. A source told Radar Online, "It's the same old Charlie. He hasn't changed at all."

Sheen's rep denied the cheating allegations, telling Radar Online, "The woman in question is the sister of one of Sheen's campmates and Sheen was only responding to a 12th step call. Since Sheen knew he was being followed and how this would look, he wore the moustache in a tongue and cheek disguise gesture." He and Mueller would file for divorce in 2010 after quite an acrimonious and disturbing history.
