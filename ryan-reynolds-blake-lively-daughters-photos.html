See Photos of Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively's Children

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively have been keeping their daughters well hidden from the spotlight for a while, but that all just changed. The proud parents just brought their little girls, one-year-old James and her newborn baby sister (they haven't revealed her name yet) to the Hollywood Walk of Fame. (What a way to make a debut.)

As expected, the children of two such genetically blessed individuals are insanely cute.

Shutterstock

Shutterstock

And as if their babies weren't cute enough, Reynolds also made this adorable statement about his wife:

"I want to thank my wife, Blake, who is sitting right there, who is everything to me. You are the best thing that has ever happened to me. You make everything better. You make everything in my life better. You've given me two of the most incredible children I've ever hoped to have."

It's an emotional time of year.
