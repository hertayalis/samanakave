Berserk Chapter 375 Release Date

You already know that the Berserk teams are working hard to reduce the delay in publishing new chapters; in keeping with this, Berserk 375 won’t be released later than Chapter 374, so fans may expect it in July.

But the most recent information indicates that Berserk Chapter 375 will be published on or before July 20, 2023. This is fantastic news for fans impatiently awaiting the next installment in the series which will air on Tuesday at 10 p.m. Japanese time.

Berserk Chapter 375 Possible Predictions Of what Will Happened

Making precise predictions about Chapter 375 is problematic. But given what happened in the previous chapters, it’s likely that attention will turn to the impending battle between Griffith’s army and Guts’ coalition of supporters. The reoccurring topic of the Beast of Darkness may also receive more attention.

It’s vital to note that Kentaro Miura, the manga’s creator, passed away tragically, yet the Berserk manga has continued. Kouji Mori, a close friend, and his principal assistant, has taken over the series and has faithfully written what Miura had asked him to without adding any extra or filler stories.

While the series’ future beyond the present arc is still unknown, it is anticipated to continue until its inevitable conclusion.
